# Privacy Policy — ListBud

**Last updated: March 2026**

---

## Summary

Your lists stay on your phone. Your voice commands briefly pass through our server to be processed and are never stored. We never sell any data.

---

## 1. Overview

ListBud is a mobile application for creating and managing lists by voice command, developed and published by an independent developer.

This privacy policy describes how ListBud collects, uses, and protects your personal data, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws.

---

## 2. Data Collected

### 2.1 Data stored locally on your device

The following data is stored exclusively on your phone and is never transmitted to us:

- The content of your lists (list names, items)
- Categories assigned to your lists
- Display colors and preferences

> ✅ **This data never leaves your device. We have no access to it.**

### 2.2 Data passing through our server

When you use the voice command feature, the text transcribed by your phone is sent to our server to be interpreted by our artificial intelligence. This processing is necessary for the application to function.

| Data | Processing |
|------|------------|
| Text of your voice command | Sent to the server for AI interpretation. Not retained after processing. |
| Name of the current list | Sent to contextualize the command. Not retained after processing. |
| Names of items in the list | Sent to enable voice checking. Not retained after processing. |
| Anonymous metrics | Action type, processing duration, error code if applicable. Retained for up to 90 days for debugging and improvement purposes. |

### 2.3 Data we do not collect

ListBud does not collect:

- Your identity or contact information — no user account required
- Your geographic location
- Your email address or phone number
- Your browsing data or advertising data
- Raw audio recordings — only the text transcription is processed

---

## 3. Purposes of Processing

Data passing through our server is used exclusively to:

- Interpret your voice commands and extract the actions to be performed
- Associate your commands with the context of your current list
- Improve the reliability and performance of the application

No processing for advertising, profiling, or resale purposes is performed.

---

## 4. Data Retention

Voice data (transcriptions, list names, items) is not retained on our server after processing. Each request is processed in real time and immediately deleted.

Anonymous metrics (action type, duration, errors) may be retained for up to 90 days for debugging and continuous improvement purposes.

Data stored locally on your device is retained until the application is uninstalled or manually deleted.

---

## 5. Hosting and Security

Our server is hosted on **Railway** (Railway Corp., United States). Requests are secured via HTTPS. Server access is protected by an API key and a request rate limiting system.

Voice command interpretation is performed via the **OpenAI API** (OpenAI, LLC, United States). Data transmitted to OpenAI is subject to their own privacy policy, available at [openai.com](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy). OpenAI commits to not using API data for training its models.

Voice transcription on **iOS** uses Apple's Speech Recognition service. On **Android**, it uses Google's speech recognition service. These services are subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

> ⛔ **We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for commercial or advertising purposes.**

Data is transmitted only to the technical service providers strictly necessary for the application to function (Railway, OpenAI), within the scope of the purposes described above.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access**: obtain a copy of the data concerning you
- **Right of rectification**: correct inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure**: request deletion of your data
- **Right to object**: object to certain processing activities
- **Right to data portability**: receive your data in a readable format

To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related questions, please contact us at the address provided in the Contact section.

Note that since most of your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time directly in the application or by uninstalling it.

---

## 8. Minors

ListBud is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your continued use of the application after any modification constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
